Simon Says Game: Rules & Prizes

How to Play:

  • Start Game: Press the "Start" button.
  • Gameplay: Follow the flashing button pattern. The sequence gets longer with each level.
  • Game Over: The game ends if you make a mistake. Your score is the level you reached.
  • Restart: Press "Start" to play again.

Prizes:

  • Level 15: Win ₹10
  • Level 30: Win ₹50

How to Claim Your Prize:

  1. Reach a prize-winning level.
  2. Take a screenshot of your screen showing the level.
  3. Send the screenshot to the developer on Instagram: @itz_shubham_arya.
  4. Once verified, your prize money will be transferred.
